ABOUT US

Hello! The products listed in the site are distributed by Global Victors.
Global Victors is a co-commerce company that was established in August 4, 2022, headquartered in Kalanki, Kathmandu, Nepal. The company operates both digitally and physically as our main focus is convenience of GV Affiliates. It is free to join as an "Affiliate" of Global Victors but to start earning with GV's co-commerce platform, you need to accumulate certain business volume/turnover as per company's terms. You join by filling a form and then work as per the system that company offers you.


We sell very good 100% natural and organic health and beauty products. We have a very wide range of Skin Care, Personal Care, Perfumes, Nepalese Tea, Dietary Supplements, Ladies and Gents Fashion wear. Our products sell themselves, because they are well known for their quality, effectiveness and healing properties.
